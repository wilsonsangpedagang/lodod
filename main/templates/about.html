About Padel In Aja

Padel In Aja is a demo web application for discovering venues, articles, and events related to padel sports. Built for learning and showcasing Django, AJAX, and pure HTML/CSS integration.

  • Browse venues, articles, and events
  • Creators can log in to publish and manage content
  • Viewers can explore without logging in
  • AJAX-powered forms and modals for seamless experience

Created by Roben for demonstration purposes.
